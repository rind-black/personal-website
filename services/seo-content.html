What Is CFD Trading? Beginner's Guide to Contracts for Difference

FinTech Beginner 6 min read

A Contract for Difference (CFD) is a financial instrument that allows traders to speculate on price movements without owning the underlying asset. CFDs are available on stocks, indices, commodities, and forex pairs.

Risk notice: CFD trading involves significant risk. Retail investor accounts lose money when trading CFDs. Ensure you understand how CFDs work before investing.

Key Benefits of CFD Trading

  • Trade long and short on the same platform
  • Access to global markets from one account
  • No stamp duty on most CFD instruments
